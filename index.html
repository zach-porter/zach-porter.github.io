Recent Master's Graduate Specializing in Generative AI

I am a recent master's graduate with a specialized focus on generative AI and its profound impact on work. My research has delved into the ways in which artificial intelligence is reshaping industries and changing the social dynamics of workers.

During my studies, I explored the ethical implications, potential risks, and the transformative power of AI technologies. My passion lies in understanding how AI can be leveraged to drive positive change while ensuring that its integration benefits workers and society. I also seek to understand how generative AI is already altering the landscape of work and how professions are adapting to these changes.

My Personal Blog: Work in the Age of AI

Welcome to my blog! Here, I explore the future of work in the age of artificial intelligence, discussing the latest trends, technologies, and research in the field. I cover topics such as the impact of AI on geopolitics, recent research advancements in generative AI, and news from businesses and organizations that develop AI hardware and software.

The rise of artificial intelligence has sparked a revolution in the workplace, transforming the way we work and interact with technology. As AI continues to evolve, it presents new opportunities and challenges at the macro-level as well as the micro-level. These changes at the different scales have implications for workers, organizations, and society as a whole.

My research has focused on the impact of AI on the future of work, exploring how generative AI technologies are reshaping industries and social relations. By understanding these changes, we can better prepare for the future and ensure that AI benefits individuals, communities, and society as a whole.
