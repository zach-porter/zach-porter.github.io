Academic Background

I have completed an MSc in Social Data Science from University College Dublin, and an MSc in Comparative Social Change from Trinity College Dublin & University College Dublin. My undergraduate degree is a BA in Communication from Hope College.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in sociological analysis and computational techniques, allowing me to tackle complex social phenomena using both qualitative and quantitative approaches.
